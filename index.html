DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The golden front frame.
Precision you can see.

Every IPETRONIK product follows a clear design language: minimalist black housing, the iconic gold front frame as the visual and functional highlight, and a highly legible, structured HMI.

01 — HOUSING
Compact. Robust. Timeless.

Minimalist black enclosures engineered for extreme environments with perfect ergonomics and long-term durability.

02 — GOLDEN FRONT FRAME
The signature element.

The precisely machined gold frame is the unmistakable visual identity and the stage for intuitive, high-quality operation.

03 — FRONT FOIL & HMI
Clarity meets performance.

Structured, highly legible interfaces designed for fast, error-free operation under real-world conditions. The M3-Series exemplifies this with its modular, intuitive front design.
